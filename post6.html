Top Affiliate Platforms in India

Affiliate marketing helps you earn by promoting other people’s products. Here are the top affiliate networks in India you should know:

  • Amazon Associates: India’s most popular program with a huge product range and trusted brand.
  • Cuelinks: Easy auto-affiliate tool with 100+ Indian merchants like Mamaearth, Nykaa, and more.
  • Impact: High-paying global and Indian brands. Great dashboard and advanced tools.
  • vCommission: One of India’s first affiliate platforms with Flipkart, AliExpress, etc.
  • ShareASale: Ideal for international affiliate programs, still supports Indian traffic.

Pro Tip: Promote affiliate links using a blog, Instagram, or WhatsApp groups to maximize earnings.
