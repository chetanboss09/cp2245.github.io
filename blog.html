Why Exercise is the Best Medicine

Posted on: 18 June 2025

Regular physical activity isn't just about building muscles or losing weight. Exercise improves your mood, boosts energy, and strengthens your heart.

Studies show that even 30 minutes of moderate activity a day can reduce the risk of chronic diseases like diabetes, hypertension, and depression.

From walking and yoga to cycling and dancing — find what you enjoy, and stick with it. The key is consistency, not intensity.

Exercise is free, powerful, and life-changing. Whether you're 16 or 60, it's never too early — or too late — to start moving!
